Trigger Warning

by Neil Gaiman

Overview

Trigger Warning is a collection of short stories and poems by Neil Gaiman, published in 2015. The collection explores the concept of warnings, the narratives that lie behind the stories, and their impact on the readers. With works ranging from horror to fantasy, and a wide array of characters and settings, this book is a brilliant example of Gaiman's ability to both entertain and unsettle his audience.

Main Characters

  • Various Characters: Each story has its own set of characters, both fictional and sometimes real-life inspired.
  • Shadow Moon: Appears in the short story “Black Dog,” a follow-up to Gaiman's *American Gods*.
  • The Sleeper and the Spindle: A story where fairy tale characters blend into a unique new narrative.

Fun Fact

Neil Gaiman has stated that the title Trigger Warning is a reference to warnings often given before disturbing content, but also hints that all stories might trigger something different in readers, whether joy, fear, or memory.
