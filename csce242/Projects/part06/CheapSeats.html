The View from the Cheap Seats

by Neil Gaiman

Overview

The View from the Cheap Seats is a collection of non-fiction writings by Neil Gaiman, featuring essays, speeches, and introductions on a wide range of topics. Gaiman covers everything from literature, art, and music to the nature of storytelling, offering insights into his creative process and experiences over the years. This book is a must-read for fans of Gaiman’s work and those curious about his thoughts on the world of art and culture.

Main Topics

  • Storytelling: Insights into the art and craft of storytelling from one of the best.
  • Pop Culture: Gaiman’s commentary on books, movies, music, and their impact on society.
  • Personal Experiences: Reflections on Gaiman's life, career, and the importance of creativity.

Fun Fact

The title The View from the Cheap Seats refers to Neil Gaiman's perspective on life and the world of art as someone who grew up loving books and stories from a distance, but is now at the center of it.
