Gory Details: Adventures from the Dark Side of Science

Overview

Gory Details: Adventures from the Dark Side of Science is a collection of weird, wonderful, and often gross stories from the darker side of scientific inquiry. The book explores topics that range from the morbidly fascinating to the truly bizarre, making it a must-read for anyone with a taste for the macabre side of science.

Main Topics

  • The Dark Side of Anatomy: Exploring bizarre anatomical curiosities.
  • Forensic Science: Fascinating cases from the world of crime-solving science.
  • The Grossest Parts of Biology: From parasites to bodily functions, biology at its most disgusting.
