Trigger Warning

by Neil Gaiman

Overview

*Trigger Warning: Short Fictions and Disturbances* is a collection of short stories and poems by Neil Gaiman, published in 2015. It includes tales of varying genres, from horror to science fiction to fantasy, exploring dark and unsettling themes that reflect human nature, mythology, and the unknown. The collection also features a novella set in the *American Gods* universe.

Notable Stories

  • The Truth Is a Cave in the Black Mountains: A story of greed, revenge, and the supernatural.
  • Orange: A humorous and mysterious story told entirely in the form of answers to an unseen questionnaire.
  • Black Dog: A novella featuring Shadow Moon from *American Gods*.
  • A Calendar of Tales: Twelve short stories, each inspired by a different month of the year.
  • Click-Clack the Rattlebag: A creepy story within a story, with an unexpected twist.

Fun Fact

Neil Gaiman has said that the story "Click-Clack the Rattlebag" was inspired by a conversation he had with a young boy while at a book signing.
