Gory Details

by Erika Engelhaupt

Overview

*Gory Details: Adventures From the Dark Side of Science* by Erika Engelhaupt explores the weird, fascinating, and often gross side of science. From bizarre animal behavior to the macabre aspects of human biology, Engelhaupt dives into the strange and curious corners of the natural world, blending humor with hard science to satisfy both the morbidly curious and scientifically inclined.

Key Topics

  • Gross Science: The science of disgust, from why we feel it to what triggers it.
  • Death and Decay: Exploring what happens to the human body after death.
  • Weird Animals: Strange animal behaviors and evolutionary oddities.
  • Forensic Science: Real-life crime scene investigations and the science behind solving them.
  • Bizarre Diseases: The world of strange and rare medical conditions.

Fun Fact

Erika Engelhaupt started her "Gory Details" series as a blog on National Geographic, where it gained a following for its quirky and sometimes cringe-inducing take on science.
